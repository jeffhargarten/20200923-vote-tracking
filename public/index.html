Minnesota 2020 Election: Tracking the vote

The 2020 general selection has started, and Minnesotans are receiving and sending back their absentee ballots. Some may also be dropping their absentee ballots off in-person at local voting centers, while other residents live in mail-only precincts.

In Minnesota's state primary this past August, most voters cast their ballots absentee as pandemic conditions drove higher levels of mail voting. Record numbers of mail ballots are expected to be counted for November's general election.
